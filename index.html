Relaxing rap to listen to.

Mijhan Gordon

Introduction

These are a random selection of calmer, more laid back rap music that I sometimes listen to. This list is in no particular order and most of these titles are explicit, though a clean version is likely available.

Star67

Drake


The production on this song in my opinion is pretty nice. I also like the overall mood of both parts of the song, especially the second verse.

Better Days

JID


This song is from one of my favourite albums in recent times. The chorus is memorable, but what I really like is the bridge and the flow of the verse after that. The production is also very strong.

Niagara Falls (Foot or 2)

Metro Boomin, Travis Scott & 21 Savage


I found this song in particular to be very underrated. The piano in the melody is catchy, as is the chorus by Travis Scott. The features compliment the production.

Hours in Silence

Drake


In my opinion one of the best recent Drake songs. The first part of the song is on the more upbeat vibe, then transitions into a slower more melodic track.

Closing

These are only some songs that I find myself coming back to when I'm either in a certain mood, or I want to distract myself from life. Although I'm able to relax to all of them, I do think they're all pretty different, and I like that kind of variety when listening to my music. Thanks for reading.
